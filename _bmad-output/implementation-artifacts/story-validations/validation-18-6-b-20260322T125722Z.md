**Benefit:** Prevents subtle visual regressions and ensures precise adherence to visual consistency requirements, contributing to a truly polished UX.

### 3. Enhance AC-5 with Visual Mimicry for Skeletons

**Benefit:** Improves the perceived loading performance and user experience by ensuring that skeletons are not merely placeholders but actively communicate the incoming content's structure.
**Source:** AC-5

**Current Gap:**

The user story's goal is to "know when content is loading" and to have the "interface feels polished and cohesive."
