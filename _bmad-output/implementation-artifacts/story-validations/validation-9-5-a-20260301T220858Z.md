### 2. Consolidate French Tax System Context

**Current:** Tax system concepts scattered across Dev Notes and task descriptions  
**Issue:** Developers unfamiliar with French tax may miss critical context

**Recommendation:** Add new subsection in Dev Notes:

```markdown
### French Tax System Context for Test Design

**Quotient Familial (Family Quotient):**
- Single person = 1 tax share
- Couple = 2 shares
- Each child adds 0.5-1 share depending on position
- **Test implication:** Family with children pays LESS tax at same income than childless couple

**Decote (Tax Reduction):** French mechanism that reduces very small tax amounts to zero
- Applied when raw tax < ~1,929 EUR
- Makes tax transition non-linear at low incomes
- **Test implication:** Zero income and 15k income may both show 0 tax due to decote

**Reference Validation:** All reference values assume OpenFisca-France is ground truth
```

**Benefit:** Developers unfamiliar with French tax can understand test design rationale.
