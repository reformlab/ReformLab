**Date:** 2026-02-25

## Research Overview

This report catalogs publicly available French datasets relevant to building an environmental policy microsimulation platform on top of OpenFisca. Focus areas: households & income, dwellings, energy equipment (heating, cooling, hot water, insulation, renewables), transport & vehicle fleet, emission factors, and geographic references. Each dataset is assessed for currentness, format, granularity, key variables, and connectability to the framework.
