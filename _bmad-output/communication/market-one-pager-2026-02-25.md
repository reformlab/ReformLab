# ReformLab — Market One-Pager

**The first self-serve, user-friendly platform for household-level environmental policy microsimulation in Europe — covering energy, transport, and biodiversity.**

---

## The Gap

Environmental policies — carbon taxes, energy subsidies, transport levies, biodiversity regulations — reshape household budgets across Europe. Yet no self-serve tool exists to model their distributional impact. Existing options are expert-only desktops (EUROMOD), US/UK-focused (PolicyEngine), or Big 4 consulting at €1,000–€2,500/day. Nothing combines an accessible interface, European environmental policy models, and open public data out of the box.

## Business Model — Open-Core Hybrid

ReformLab follows the WordPress/Automattic playbook:

- **Open-source core** builds trust with government and academic buyers
- **Hosted SaaS** generates recurring revenue as the product matures
- **Consulting and training** produce cash flow from day one — bridging 6–18 month government procurement cycles
- **Grants** (Horizon Europe, BPI France, ADEME) fund R&D without equity dilution

| Revenue Stream | Launch Phase | Steady State |
| -------------- | ------------ | ------------ |
| Grants (EU, ADEME, BPI France) | €30K–€80K | €50K–€100K |
| Consulting (policy analysis projects) | €20K–€50K | €100K–€200K |
| SaaS subscriptions | €0–€5K | €30K–€100K |
| Training and workshops | €5K–€10K | €20K–€50K |
| **Total** | **€55K–€145K** | **€200K–€450K** |

SaaS gross margin reaches ~90% at 100 paying users.

## Cost to Launch

Year 1 cash cost (solo build): **€2,700–€9,600** — hosting, AI APIs, admin, and accounting. Adding freelance frontend and design help raises it to €20K–€40K.

## Competitive Edge

- **OpenFisca backbone** — proven French tax-benefit engine, maintained by an active open-source association
- **No-code interface** — governments and think tanks run scenarios without Python skills
- **Open-data-first** — works out of the box with public datasets (INSEE, Eurostat, EU-SILC)
- **Environmental policy focus** — energy, transport, biodiversity — the one niche nobody else serves with a self-serve tool
