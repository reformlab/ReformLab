# Communication Outputs

This folder stores human-facing deliverables you can share directly with collaborators, prospects, and other stakeholders.

## Included Files

- `audience-narratives-2026-02-25.md` — Story pack adapted for public, expert/institutional, and investor audiences.
- `email-outreach-french-2026-02-25.md` — Ready-to-send French outreach emails and adaptation notes.
- `market-one-pager-2026-02-25.md` — Executive one-pager distilling market research into a skimmable overview for advisors and funders.

## Positioning vs Research Files

- `_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/research/` is research-first material (evidence gathering and analysis inputs).
- `_bmad-output/communication/` is audience-ready material (packaged messaging and outreach assets).
